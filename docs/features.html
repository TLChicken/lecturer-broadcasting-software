Draw Anywhere on your Screen!

Toggle Drawing Mode with CTRL + SHIFT!


Switch on Drawing Mode to begin creating annotations on top of your apps, anywhere on your screen!

When Drawing Mode is on, the button would be colored bright green.


Use Left Click to start annotating on your screen! During this time you cannot interact with your apps underneath the overlay. You can only click on or type on the apps under the overlay when Drawing Mode is OFF


To quickly switch between annotating on top of your apps and interacting with the apps below your screen, use CTRL + SHIFT (hold down CTRL then press SHIFT) to switch Drawing Mode between OFF and ON


Please also see: Important Notes about how Drawing Mode works


Multiple Brushes!

Pen Brush (P)

Highlighter Brush (H)

Eraser Brush (E)


Select a brush to use for drawing! The selected brush will be highlighted blue even if Drawing Mode is not on.

Customise your Brushes!

Choose from your palette of colors!


Click the Color Palette button to view your available colors.

Click on one of the colors to select it for your brush!

Custom colors can be set in the Settings Menu.

The current color is saved even if LAT is closed and reopened.

Change brush size!


  • Conveniently use your mouse scroll wheel while in Drawing Mode to increase and decrease your brush sizes.
  • The Eraser brush size is saved separately from the brush size for all your other brushes!
  • Brush sizes are saved even when LAT is closed and reopened.
  • Brush sizes can also be set in the "Other" section of the Settings Menu. (Coming Soon!)
  • The brush outline shown while in Drawing Mode would also reflect the size of the current brush. (Coming Soon!)
