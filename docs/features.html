Keyboard shortcuts can only be activated while Drawing Mode is on except for the 3 that are colored purple.

Draw Anywhere on your Screen!

Toggle Drawing Mode with CTRL + SHIFT!


Switch on Drawing Mode to begin creating annotations on top of your apps, anywhere on your screen!

When Drawing Mode is on, the button would be colored bright green.


Use Left Click to start annotating on your screen! During this time you cannot interact with your apps underneath the overlay. You can only click on or type on the apps under the overlay when Drawing Mode is OFF


To quickly switch between annotating on top of your apps and interacting with the apps below your screen, use CTRL + SHIFT (hold down CTRL then press SHIFT) to switch Drawing Mode between OFF and ON


Please also see: Important Notes about how Drawing Mode works


Multiple Brushes!

Pen Brush (P)

Highlighter Brush (H)

Eraser Brush (E)


Select a brush to use for drawing! The selected brush will be highlighted blue even if Drawing Mode is not on.

Customise your Brushes!

Choose from your palette of colors!


Click the Color Palette button to view your available colors.

Click on one of the colors to select it for your brush!

Custom colors can be set in the Settings Menu.

The current color is saved even if LAT is closed and reopened.

Change brush size!


  • Conveniently use your mouse scroll wheel while in Drawing Mode to increase and decrease your brush sizes.
  • The Eraser brush size is saved separately from the brush size for all your other brushes!
  • Brush sizes are saved even when LAT is closed and reopened.
  • Brush sizes can also be set in the "Other" section of the Settings Menu. (Coming Soon!)
  • The brush outline shown while in Drawing Mode would also reflect the size of the current brush. (Coming Soon!)

Customise your Colors!

Choose from preset Color Themes!


  • Use the Color Theme Dropdown to quickly set all the colors in your palette to follow your favourite theme!

  • Find it in the Color Tab of the Settings Menu.
  • 3 color themes currently available!
  • View the available color themes below!

Available Color Themes:

Original

Pastel

Darks

Set your own custom colors!


  • Set any custom color that you want in your palette!
  • Select a color inside the Color Tab of the Settings Menu to manually set its color.
  • Set it to any color by inputting your desired RGB values!
  • Or use a color selector to choose a color that you want more quickly!

Customise all your Keybinds!

Change any of the keybinds available in LAT to your liking!


  • Freely change any of LAT's default keybinds to any key that you want!
  • Click any keybind inside the Keybinds Tab of the Settings Menu to change it.
  • The selected keybind would be highlighted in red, press any key on your keyboard to set it.
  • LAT automatically stops you from setting keybinds that conflict with other LAT keybinds!

Erase All Convenience Button

Quickly clear all your annotations!


  • Use the Erase All button to quickly remove all the annotations on your screen!
  • Alternatively, press the Delete key instead of clicking the button to get the same outcome.

Screenshots!

Save a Screenshot with your annotations really quickly!


  • Press the Save/Screenshot button anytime to very quickly take a snapshot of your screen together with the annotations!
  • The new screenshot would automatically be saved in your Downloads folder with the current timestamp as the filename.
  • The LAT Toolbar would be hidden before the screenshot is taken so that it does not block your screen!
  • Change the default save location of the screenshot in the Others Tab of the Settings Menu. (Coming Soon!)

Get the Toolbar out of the way!

Minimize the toolbar and use LAT's keyboard shortcuts to make fuller use of your screen while annotating!


  • Press the Minimise button anytime to compact the toolbar into a small square that only contains a Maximize button.
  • In this way, you can make use of LAT's keyboard shortcuts to quickly draw annotations without the LAT toolbar taking up any of your screen's real estate!
  • The Maximize button would be colored Dark Green or Light Green corresponding to whether you are in Drawing Mode or not.
  • Click the Maximise button to turn the LAT Toolbar back into the full sized version!

  • EXTRA! Use the alternative CTRL + M keyboard shortcut instead of the Minimize button to FULLY hide the LAT Toolbar away from your screen. (Experimental!)

Slideshow Integration! (Coming Soon)

Automatically save annotations of your slides!


  • Toggle the Slideshow button to ON to start Slideshow Recording Mode!
  • Turn it on when you begin your PowerPoint slideshow or when presenting any other kind of slide deck!
  • Freely annotate on your slides using LAT's tools.
  • When you press the Right Arrow key to go to the next slide, LAT would automatically save a screenshot of the current slide together with your complete annotations, before the next slide is shown.
  • After your slideshow has ended, find the compiled screenshots of your slides complete with your annotations in your Downloads folder!
