Supports both Windows and Mac!

Live Annotation Tools is a desktop overlay app that allows you to draw anywhere on your screen! Various brushes and tools are included to help you make drawings quick and easily – Perfect for live presentations!

This app's main focus is on helping diversify the annotation tools university lecturers have available when conducting their lessons (both in-person and remote!), however, its usage can be extended to any type of live presentation as well.

Draw anywhere on your screen!

Live Annotation Tools uses an overlay on top of all your other apps, so that you can annotate on anything and everything that you want!

Tools Customisability!

Live Annotation Tools is packed full of customisability! Change your brush size easily, choose a custom color, set your own comfortable keybinds for your presentation needs!

Optimised for LIVE Presentations!

Unlike PowerPoint’s default presenter view, Live Annotation Tools makes it very easy to change to your desired color or change brushes with a press of your keyboard!

Save annotations automatically!

After toggling Presenter mode to ON in Live Annotation Tools, screenshots of the current slide and annotations would be compiled each time you press your desired “Next Slide Button” to go to the next slide in PowerPoint. (Coming Soon!)
